A forensic guide to Satellite Navigation and GPS Untis

This section will highlight the data that can be gathered from Sat-Nav units.

The importance of Sat-Nav units in a forensic investigation relating to vehicles.

Sat Nav and GPS units can be a vital source of data for forensic investigators, as they contain a range of different user location data, often with timestamps. Below we highlgight some of the data available from these units.

Data that can be recovered.

  • ✅ Home addresses
  • ✅ Recent and favourite locations
  • ✅ Contacts and call registers (for some models)
  • ✅ Route history

The goal of extracting data out of Sat-Navs.

The aim behind extracting and analysing Sat-Nav data is to allow investigators to reconstruct the travel history of a suspect or a victim. This data can provide a valuable insight into where someone has been, information that may not be available otherwise.
